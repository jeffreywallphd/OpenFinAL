# Product Backlog Refinement

> When do Product Backlog Refinement meetings occur? At the beginning of every sprint
Product Backlog Refinement Meetings should only last **10-15 minutes.**
* * *
**Facilitator:** SCRUM Master
**Backup-Facilitator:** Product Owner
**Second Backup:** Team Lead
* * *
### Meeting Details
**Date:** x/xx/xx
**Cadence:** Every 2 Weeks
**Time Taken:** X minutes
*   Who led today's meeting?
* * *
### Current Sprint Backlog Items
*   Discuss what items should be updated in the product backlog currently
*   Update and remove any existing user stories as needed
    *   Note: If this is the first meeting, figure out what items should be added to the backlog in the first place and why they are important.
* * *
### Upcoming Sprint Backlog Items
*       *   Discuss what items or **new** user stories should be added
    *   Figure out what items should be added to the backlog in the first place and why they are important
* * *
### Estimate & Document Backlog Items
*       *   Discuss an estimation on how long each product backlog item will take
        *   Will it get done in the current sprint?
        *   Should it get moved to the next sprint?
        *   Can this task be broken down?
            *   If so, what can be completed during this sprint?
        *   What sprints will these broken down tasks get done in? (1-5)
