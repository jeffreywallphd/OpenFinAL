# Sprint Review

> When do Sprint Review meetings occur? At the end of every sprint
Sprint Review Meetings should only last **10-15 minutes.**
* * *
**Facilitator:** SCRUM Master
**Backup-Facilitator:** Product Owner
**Second Backup:** Team Lead
* * *
### Meeting Details
**Date:** x/xx/xx
**Cadence:** Every 2 Weeks
**Time Taken:** X minutes
*   Who led today's meeting?
* * *
### Team Sprint Goals
*   What were the goals for this Sprint?

* * *
### Sprint Review Questions
*       *   What goals were met?
    *   What goals were not met?
    *   What goals should be added for the next sprint?
* * *
### Completed Tasks
- [ ] Review task list and mark which tasks are "Done" and "Not Done"
- [ ] Follow up on the "Not Done" tasks to add notes on any blockers
* * *
### Product Backlog
- [ ] Update the Product Backlog
*   What tasks should change in priority?
*   What tasks should be added to the product backlog?
*   What tasks should be removed from the product backlog for the upcoming Sprint?
