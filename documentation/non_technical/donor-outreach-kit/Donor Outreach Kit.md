# **Epic: Donor Outreach Kit**

**Goal:** Equip the OpenFinAl team with materials, tools, and processes to identify, approach, and convert potential donors/partners.

---

##  **Feature 1 — Target Donor Research**

### **Outcome**

A qualified list of donor prospects with prioritization.

**Tasks**

* Define ideal donor profile (ICP)

* Identify donor segments (foundations, fintech sponsors, universities)

* Build prospect database (CRM or spreadsheet)

* Gather contact information

* Score and prioritize prospects

* Validate list with stakeholders

---

##  **Feature 2 — Outreach Messaging & Positioning**

### **Outcome**

Clear, consistent messaging for donor communication.

**Tasks**

* Define value proposition for donors

* Draft outreach narrative (mission \+ impact)

* Create elevator pitch

* Write email outreach templates

* Write LinkedIn message templates

* Create call script

* Internal review & approval

---

##  **Feature 3 — Donor Pitch Materials**

### **Outcome**

Professional assets to support outreach.

**Tasks**

* Create pitch deck

* Design one-pager overview

* Develop impact metrics slide

* Prepare FAQ document

* Build demo script

* Review branding consistency

---

##  **Feature 4 — Outreach Execution Process**

### **Outcome**

Repeatable process for contacting donors.

**Tasks**

* Define outreach workflow (research → contact → follow-up)

* Set cadence for follow-ups

* Configure CRM tracking

* Assign outreach ownership

* Create status tracking dashboard

* Document SOP

---

## **Feature 5 — Measurement & Optimization**

### **Outcome**

Ability to evaluate outreach effectiveness.

**Tasks**

* Define success metrics (response rate, meetings booked, funding secured)

* Build tracking sheet/dashboard

* Conduct weekly outreach review

* Identify messaging improvements

* Iterate templates

---

# **Example Deliverables for the Epic**

* Prospect list

* Messaging playbook

* Pitch deck

* Outreach templates

* Outreach tracking dashboard

---

#  **Definition of Done**

* Team has ready-to-use outreach materials

* Prospect pipeline exists

* Outreach process documented
