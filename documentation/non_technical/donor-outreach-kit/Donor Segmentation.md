**Donor Segments**

1. ## **Foundations & Nonprofits**

   **Who they are:** Organizations that fund education, financial literacy, and technology access.  
     
   **Examples:**   
* Charles Schwab Foundation  
* FINRA Investor Education Foundation  
* Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation  
* Kauffman Foundation

	**Why do they give:**

* Promote financial literacy  
* Support innovative education programs  
* Scale impact across communities

2. ## **FinTech & Financial Services Sponsors**

   **Who they are:** Companies that benefit from fintech innovations and talent pipelines.  
     
   **Examples (high-fit):**  
* Stripe  
* Plaid  
* Robinhood  
* SoFi  
* Intuit

	  
	**Why they give:** 

* Early access to student talent  
* Brand visibility on campus  
* Align with innovation \+ education

3. ## **University & Alumni Networks**

   **Who they are:** Internal and alumni-based funding sources  
     
   **Examples:**   
* Michigan Technological University Alumni Association  
* Michigan Tech Fund  
* College of Business donors/ advisory boards


	**Why they give:** 

* School pride  
* Supporting student success  
* Giving back to their program

4. ## **Local & Regional Businesses**

   **Who they are:** Smaller companies, banks, and professionals  
     
   **Examples:**   
* Local credit unions  
* Regional banks  
* Financial advisors/wealth managers  
* Tech startups in Michigan

	**Why they give:**

* Community Impact  
* Local visibility  
* Recruiting local talent

**How to use this:** 

1. **Start with “Warm” Leads (Highest Success)**  
   1. Alumni  
   2. Professors’ connections  
   3. Local businesses  
2. **Then Move to “Cold but High Value”**  
   1. FinTech companies  
   2. Foundations  
3. **Customize Messaging by Segment**  
   1. Foundations → *impact \+ education*  
   2. FinTech → *innovation \+ talent pipeline*  
   3. Alumni → *“You were in our shoes once.”*
